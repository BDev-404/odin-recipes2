Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a perfect dish for year round enjoyment. Bringing back the homestyle cooking that people love and delivering a from scratch meal with minimal prepwork and effort. The beauty of pasta an sauce continues to amaze me!

Ingredients

  • Meat:For a great lasagna I recommend a blend of ground veal and ground hamburger. But you can always just use some time proven 80/20 ground hamburger!
  • Onion and Garlic:For your onion, I recommend a basic white onion. You'll want about 1/2 onions per person you aim to serve. Fresh garlic is best, but to save time minced will work as well.
  • Tomato Products:You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and a 16oz can of tomato paste.
  • Spices and Seasonings:This recipe is going to use fresh parsley, dired basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles:I recommend bronze cut lasagna, Aldi has a great selection. Any store bought lasagna works.
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make a rich melody.
  • Egg:An egg will help bind the cheese to have a more consistent structure.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce:Cook up your blend of meat in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook until mostly brown, then add your seasoning. Lastly add your diced tomatoes and sauce, simmer until incorporated and lastly blend in the paste.
  2. Cook the Noodles:Boil in a 4qt sauce pan, bring 2qts water to a boil with a generous amount of salt. Cook about 12 minutes or until aldente.
  3. Ricotta:Warm the ricotta to make it more maleable, incorporate the egg with some italian seasoning and set asside.
  4. Layer the Lasagna:With a light layer of sauce on your pan, layer up in the order of noodles, ricotta, mozzarella slices,meat sauce, parm, and repeat.
  5. Baking:Preheat your oven to 375 degrees, cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes. Then let it cook uncovered for 25 minutes.
  6. Let it set for 15minutes and then enjoy!
